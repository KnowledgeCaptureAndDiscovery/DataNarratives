Data Narrative 3: Method view (Show more) (Show less)

Reaeration empirical performs two main types of analyses in the initial dataset. The RaerationEmpirical step is the step that compares the Dissolved Oxygen, Temperature, Depth, and Velocity to determine the rate of reaeration, or the K2 value. The relationship between these inputs is determined through regression on past data. Reaeration is the process by which oxygen is absorbed by the stream from the air. After applying the HourlyAverages step, the Metabolism step is executed to produce the target results.

Data Narrative 4: Abstract view (Show more) (Show less)

The Reaeration empirical method has 3 steps. The first one, Waterflow_Hourly_Averages step, uses an input dataset (WaterflowInputs) to produce a result. Next, the Raeration_Empirica step consumes that output to produce another result. The final step is the metabolisma step, which produced the Metabolism_03-02-2010 results.

Data Narrative 5: Implementation view (Show more) (Show less)

Reaeration empirical performs two main types of analyses in the initial dataset. The RaerationEmpirical step compares the Dissolved Oxygen, Temperature, Depth, and Velocity to determine the rate of reaeration, or the K2 value. The relationship between these inputs is determined through regression on past data. Reaeration is the process by which oxygen is absorbed by the stream from the air. The rearation empirical step uses the ReaerationCMNode software implementation.

After applying the HourlyAverages step, the Metabolism step is executed to produce the target results. The metabolism step uses the MetabolismDay software implementation.
